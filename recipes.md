Dice:
 - [ ] 3 celery sticks
 - [ ] 3 carrots
 - [ ] 2 onions
 - [ ] 200g bacon

Cooked diced stuff in olive oil and:
 - [ ] butter

Add:
 - [ ] 1kg wallaby mince

Add:
 - [ ] fresh rosemary and/or thyme

When mince is frying (liquid gone), add:
 - [ ] 300g tomato paste
 - [ ] 2 cups chicken stock

When cooked, serve with:
 - [ ] shredded cheese
 - [ ] pasta
